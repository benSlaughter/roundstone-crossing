# Camera site recordings

Each subdirectory holds a single recording session from a candidate camera site.

## Naming convention
`site_NN_YYYY-MM-DD` — sequential site number + date of recording.

## Per-site contents
- `*.avi` (or `*.mp4`) — raw footage from the camera
- `site.yaml` — site metadata: position, angle, ROI coordinates, notes
- `frames/` — extracted frames (only generated by the analysis script — gitignored)
- `detections.csv` — per-tick barrier/wig-wag state (output of analysis)
- `quality_report.md` — human-readable site quality assessment
- `quality.json` — machine-readable quality metrics for cross-site comparison

## Files NOT to commit
Raw video files and extracted frames are large and may include identifiable
imagery — keep them out of git. The `.gitignore` excludes `*.avi`, `*.mp4`,
and `frames/` under this directory. Commit only `site.yaml`, `detections.csv`
and the quality reports.
